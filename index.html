Sinnoh Lore Deck

The Temporal Deity: Dialga

Steel Dragon

History & Origin

According to myths found in ancient Sinnoh structures, Dialga was created by the original entity alongside Palkia and Giratina. When Dialga came into being, it is said that the flow of time began its continuous journey forward. It commands temporal lines and remains asleep in a dimension completely detached from reality.

Chemistry & Typings

Unlike common Dragon types exposed to ice and fairy weaknesses, Dialga possesses a unique metallic composition. Its part Steel structural profile alters its molecular defenses, rendering structural armor that counters traditional dragon weaknesses. It stands as a resilient sovereign capable of processing timelines like clockwork mechanics.

The Deep Lake Keepers

The Lake Trio

Sinnoh's mythical guardians created to balance the spirit of humanity.

Uxie

Psychic

The Being of Knowledge | Lake Acuity

Known as the bringer of wisdom, it is said that its emergence gave humanity the intellect to solve problems and improve their lives. It keeps its eyes strictly closed, as myth says looking into them wipes away one's memory instantly.

Mesprit

Psychic

The Being of Emotion | Lake Verity

This playful guardian taught humans the nobility of sorrow, pain, and joy. Unlike the others, it leaves its lake cavern to playfully roam across the Sinnoh region once awakened, testing a trainer's heart.

Azelf

Psychic

The Being of Willpower | Lake Valor

It endowed humans with the determination needed to face hardships. It maintains the world's internal balance by sleeping at the bottom of its lake; should it be harmed, it can immobilize a target's free will permanently.
